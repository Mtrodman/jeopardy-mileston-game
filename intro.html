How to play

  • This quiz will test you on processing questions

  • Once you are at the quiz the first of 15 questions will show .

  • Each qustion is multiple choice when you decided on the answer you think is correct click on the button to select that answer. Once you have selected an answer the quiz will proceed to the next question

  • Once you have completed all of the questions you will get your final score and it will show the number of questions you got right out of 15. To see what the correct answers are click on the check answers button. If you want to retry the quiz select the back to quiz button.

  • When you are ready to test your knowledge click the "Proceed To Quiz" button at the bottom you will be taken to the Quiz.
