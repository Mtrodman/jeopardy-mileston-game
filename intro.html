Welcome to Call of Duty Jeopardy

Game Instructions

  • This will test your knowledge on 5 different Call of Duty Games

  • Under each catergoy are 5 questions ranging from easy to hard

  • Once you decide which tile you want click on the tile and the question will pop up if you have answered correctly it will turn the answer green and increase your score if you answered wrong it will show red and your score wont change

  • You will have 3 attempts at testing your knowledge before the game will end and display your final score

  • When your ready to test your knowledge and see how high of score you can get click the start button to proceed to the game
